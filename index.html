About

The short version: engineer by training, DevOps by choice, still early — and fine with that.

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering graduate at St Thomas College of Engineering & Technology, Chengannur, currently completing hands-on DevOps training at Zoople Technologies. I got here through building things — full-stack apps, IoT systems, a bit of AI/ML — and realizing the part I enjoyed most was making systems reliable, automated, and easy to ship.

Right now that means going deep on Linux, Git, Bash scripting, AWS fundamentals (EC2), Ansible and Terraform, while continuing to explore Docker and the wider CI/CD toolchain. I'm not further along than that — and I'd rather say so than pad a resume. What I can promise is that I learn fast, document what I build, and don't disappear when something breaks.

Outside the terminal, I've spent three years in student leadership — chairing my college union and IEEE student branch — which mostly taught me how to keep a lot of moving parts coordinated under a deadline. Turns out that's useful in DevOps too.
